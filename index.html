Accommodation Options

Sanctuaries of the Island

Langkawi offers a variety of peaceful 5-star settings. We recommend these locations for their commitment to stillness and natural beauty.

The Danna

Colonial-style elegance at Telaga Harbour. Known for its massive infinity pool and classic, timeless architecture.

The Datai

Hidden deep within an ancient rainforest. Ideal for total immersion in nature and quiet, earthy luxury.

Four Seasons

Located on the pristine Tanjung Rhu beach. A vast, pavilion-style resort that offers unmatched space and privacy.

The Ritz-Carlton

Modern luxury blended with the jungle. Features a world-class overwater spa and private beach cove.

Casa del Mar

A boutique "Home by the Sea" on Pantai Cenang. Small, intimate, and highly personalized service.

The St. Regis

The height of sophistication on the island's southern tip. Perfect for those who enjoy refined, grand spaces.

The Westin

A wellness-focused resort near the ferry terminal. Excellent spa facilities and restorative "Heavenly" beds.

Pelangi Beach

Traditional Malay village architecture set in lush tropical gardens. Very authentic and family-friendly.

Tanjung Rhu Resort

A quiet classic on the most beautiful beach in Langkawi. Known for its peaceful, wide-open spaces.

Ambong Pool Villas

Private, eco-conscious villas perched on a hillside. Offers incredible views and a deep sense of seclusion.

Common Inquiries

Clarity for Your Journey

Click on a question to reveal the answer.

Is this retreat suitable for beginners? +
Absolutely. Our yoga sessions are slow-paced and restorative, and our horse riding trails are guided by professionals who cater to first-time riders. This journey is about connection, not performance.
What exactly is the "KL Exclusive" add-on? +
For guests in the Klang Valley, we provide a true door-to-door experience. This includes a private car from your home to the airport, flight coordination, and a warm welcome upon arrival in Langkawi. We handle the transitions so you can remain in a state of rest.
What happens if it rains during our activities? +
Langkawi’s tropical rhythm includes passing showers. If weather affects our outdoor plans, we shift to indoor forest-view yoga, wellness workshops, or allow for extra quiet time in the sanctuary of your resort.
Are flights included in the investment? +
Our standard rates cover the retreat flow, accommodation, meals, and island transport. For those choosing the KL Exclusive add-on, we assist in coordinating and including your flights for a seamless end-to-end package.
Can I book for a group or corporate team? +
Yes. We specialize in boutique group experiences and end-to-end corporate retreats. Please reach out via our Corporate section for a custom quotation tailored to your team's size and goals.
